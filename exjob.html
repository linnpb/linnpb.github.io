Instagram Privacy Policy

Degree Project in Computer Science and Engineering, specializing in Interactive Media Technology



🛠

  • User research by holding interviews
  • Prototyping in Figma
  • User testing

The goal of this project was to design a more usable privacy policy for Instagram. During the process, I followed the double diamond model, starting with framing the problem by holding user interviews. To create the prototype, I used the design tool Figma. The final design of the prototype was created iteratively after the feedback I got from the user tests.
