CAI

Media Lab



🛠

  • User research by holding interviews
  • Prototyping in Marvel
  • User testing

In this project, the purpose was to define a problem and come up with a solution. Research and interviews were conducted to be able to define the problem and the target group. We decided to focus on smaller companies in their marketing process on social media. Our solution was an artificial intelligent content generator called CAI. To present our idea, a prototype was designed in Marvel.
