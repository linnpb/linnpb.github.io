Graphic Components and its Impact on Trust

Degree Project in Media Technology



🛠

  • Prototyping in Marvel
  • User testing

In this project, we wanted to understand how to build trust by the quality and design of a website, and how the layout and content shaping increases or decreases user trust. The goal was to look closer at the impact of the placement of graphic components such as product title, product price, the purchase button, and the navigation menu on a product page of a webshop. To determine this, participants ranked prototypes, which I made in Marvel, in which the placement of the graphic components varied.
