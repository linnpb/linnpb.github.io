Bloody Quarters

Media Technology and Interaction Design



🛠

  • Creating personas and scenarios
  • User Flow/Wireframing
  • Prototyping in Marvel
  • User Testing

During this design project, we went from an idea to a functional prototype by following the double diamon model. Personas and scenarios helped us defined the game Bloody Quarter, which is an interactive horror story with social parts inspired by PokemonGo and Geocaching. Lo-fi storyboard sketches helped us create the user flow for the prototype. The prototype, which I had the main responsability for, was made in Marvel. The prototype was user-tested and refined after feedback.
